# Indian Job Market Analysis Dashboard (2025–2026)

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes the Indian job market during 2025–2026. The dashboard transforms raw employment data into meaningful insights, helping users understand hiring trends, salary distributions, skill demand, and regional job opportunities.

The objective is to provide a comprehensive view of the evolving job market and support data-driven decision-making for students, professionals, recruiters, and organizations.

---

## Problem Statement

The Indian job market is changing rapidly due to technological advancements, digital transformation, and evolving industry requirements. Understanding these changes requires analyzing large volumes of employment-related data.

This dashboard addresses that challenge by visualizing:

- Job demand across industries
- Location-wise hiring trends
- Salary patterns
- Emerging skill requirements
- Workforce market dynamics
